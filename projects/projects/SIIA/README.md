# Smart Invoice Investigation Assistant (SIIA)

## Overview

Smart Invoice Investigation Assistant (SIIA) is an AI-powered enterprise product designed to help Accounts Payable and Procurement teams investigate invoice exceptions using AI-driven root cause analysis and actionable recommendations.

## Problem Statement

Finance users often spend significant time investigating invoice exceptions and depend on technical support teams to identify configuration issues, tax mismatches, or master data problems. This leads to delayed invoice processing and increased operational effort.

## Vision

Enable finance users to resolve invoice exceptions independently through AI-powered investigation and intelligent recommendations.

## Target Users

- Accounts Payable Specialist
- Procurement Specialist
- Finance Consultant
- SAP Support Consultant
